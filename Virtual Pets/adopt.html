How to care for your virtual pets

It is important to pay close attention to your pet's needs. In particular, it is vital that you:

  • Feed your pet at least once a day
  • Interact with your pet frequently
  • Schedule regular grooming for your pet
  • Wash your pet
  • Reward your pet's good behaviour
  • Teach your pet tricks
