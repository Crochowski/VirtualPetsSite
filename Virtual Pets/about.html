Virtual Pet Co was founded by Figgs Blueshell in 2012. Mr Blueshell had a hard life growing up on the streets and had to survive by playing harmonica in various blues bands in Harlem. Blueshell's talent became renowned across the globe and he eventually earned his place among other legendary jazz greats. Using his well-earned fortune, he started up Blueshell's Buddies to ensure that no other animals would have to endure the hardships growing up that he had.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often do pets need to be fed?

Pets should be fed once a day.

Can I adopt more than one pet?

Of course! We are always delighted for a pet to find a home. Adopt as many as you like!

Can I return my pet?

We hope this will not be necessary, but yes.

Do the pets get delivered to my house?

The pets are virtual, and cannot survive outside the internet. You are free to see them online whenever you like!

How much do pets cost?

The pets are not for sale, they are for adoption. There's no charge!

Do the pets talk?

No, the pets do not talk. However, The Colonel can screech quite loudly.

How many pets are available?

There are currently 4 available pets.

Are there plans to provide more pets?

Yes, we are soon expecting to receive several more pets so check the site frequently!

May I be a pet?

No.
